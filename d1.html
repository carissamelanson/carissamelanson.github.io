Project Brief

D1 is a college readiness app designed to help student athletes navigate the college recruitment process. My team explored and refined the concept for this project during the ATX Diversity Hackathon. I collaborated with developers to create pixel perfect mockups for the app and used Marvel to prototype it.

Tools Used

Sketching, Prototyping, Illustrator, Marvel

Process

After refining the concept, we created user personas and user stories to gain insight about the app's target audience. I created a style tile to explore colors and typography to create a look and feel for the app that would connect with student athletes.
